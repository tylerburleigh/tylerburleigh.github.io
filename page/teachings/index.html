I’ve given talks and lectures.

Psychology talks

2019/04 - A whirlwind tour of persuasion psychology

An educational talk for a non-academic audience about the foundations of persuasion psychology. Covers human evolution, irrationality, heuristics and biases, cognitive dissonance, and social norms.

2017/11 - What and how we measure things in psychology

An educational talk for a non-academic audience about measurements in psychology. Sort of a 101 on measurement: What a measurement is, how a measurement comes to be created, and why we measure things at all. Followed by a deper dive into the measurement of emotions.

2017/07 - Predicting and preventing depression episodes

This talk was given to a non-academic audience and was designed partly to educate about depression and predictive algorithms, and partly to get people excited about the potential of predictive algorithms in this domain.
